Post Graduate Hello Industry Experience
Capstone projects for Coursework Masters students in Faculty of Information Technology

Overview

With innovation and community service at its heart, Monash Faculty of IT Masters students, guided by industry and academic mentors, are developing IT applications to address societal issues, using publicly available big data to drive their thinking.

Programme Details

Each semester, students from diverse IT backgrounds working in multicultural teams, are immersed in a unique industry experience. With an innovative and entrepreneurial mindset, teams analyse data to develop IT solutions (apps and websites) to real-life problems and/or identified opportunities presented by the analysis. Students are pushed beyond the comfort of their discipline boundaries and current knowledge, as they not only practise their craft in an industry setting, but also develop a sense of service.

Throughout this process the teams are mentored by expert, enthusiastic industry mentors, who play a key role coaching, questioning and providing feedback. They advise on product and process, share their industry experience and help their teams avoid pitfalls.

The program’s success is evident from the students’ palpable pride in their achievement, which comes from being challenged to surpass self-imposed limits. This pride is shared by industry who are excited to be guiding the next generation of IT professionals, and collaborating in a program committed to community service.
